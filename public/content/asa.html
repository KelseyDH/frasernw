ASA is no longer indicated for primary prevention of cardiovascular events

Cardiology

This 2011 meta-analysis included more than 100,000 patients. For every 1,000 subjects treated with aspirin over a 5-year period, aspirin would prevent 2.9 major coronary events and cause 2.8 major bleeds.
