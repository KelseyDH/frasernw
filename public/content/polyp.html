GI Polyp Results

Gastroenterology

Hyperplastic Polyps

Not a concern

Adenomatous Polyps

Lesser concern if:

  • tubular
  • low grade
  • small
  • completely removed

Increased concern if:

  • villous
  • high grade
  • larger
  • incompletely removed

1/10 adenomatous polyps can progress to cancer, so 90% do not progress.

5 yr follow up scope usually recommended. Recommendation depends on comorbidity and patients life expectancy etc.
